About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at DAV University, Jalandhar, India. I hold a Ph.D. from Guru Nanak Dev University (Amritsar) where I focussed on designing Nature-Inspired Metaheuristic Algorithms for Feature Selection. Over the years I have built research programs combining metaheuristic optimization with machine learning and deep learning for medical image analysis (breast mammography, retinal imaging, chest X-ray and MRI).

My work spans algorithm design (nature-inspired metaheuristics), feature selection for high-dimensional medical image data, and practical deep learning pipelines for disease diagnosis. I have collaborated internationally (including with Prof. Uwe Aickelin, University of Melbourne) on projects in diabetic retinopathy and diagnostic model optimization.
