About Me

I am currently working as Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at DAV University, Jalandhar, India. I completed my Ph.D. at Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar, India specializing in Nature-Inspired Metaheuristic Algorithms for Feature Selection in Machine Learning. My recent collaboration with Prof. Uwe Aickelin (University of Melbourne) contributed to optimizing diabetic retinopathy diagnoses using advanced machine learning techniques. I am keen on research opportunities in Machine Learning and Deep Learning focused on precise diagnosis of diseases in medical images.
