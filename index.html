About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at DAV University, Jalandhar, India. With over a decade of teaching and research experience, I have specialized in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Metaheuristic Algorithms, and Medical Image Analysis.

My Ph.D. research focused on designing innovative Nature-Inspired Metaheuristic Algorithms for Feature Selection. One of my most impactful works, in collaboration with Prof. Uwe Aickelin (University of Melbourne), contributed significantly to improving diabetic retinopathy diagnosis using AI-based optimization models.

My research focuses on advancing Artificial Intelligence by integrating deep learning, machine learning, and nature-inspired metaheuristic optimization techniques to solve complex problems in medical imaging and healthcare diagnostics . I aim to design innovative computational models that not only achieve high predictive accuracy but are also interpretable and reliable for real-world medical use. A central focus of my work is on developing AI-driven solutions for early disease detection, including diabetic retinopathy, breast cancer, and respiratory illnesses, thereby contributing to improved patient outcomes and assisting healthcare professionals in clinical decision-making. In addition to algorithmic development, I am committed to enhancing the robustness, generalizability, and explainability of AI systems. By combining optimization algorithms with transfer learning and vision transformer-based architectures, my long-term goal is to create adaptive diagnostic frameworks capable of working across diverse medical datasets and clinical environments. My research vision extends toward interdisciplinary collaborations between computer science and medicine, with the ultimate aim of creating AI systems that are not only technologically advanced but also ethically responsible and clinically deployable. My teaching aims to cultivate critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and research-oriented learning in students of computer science and engineering. I focus on creating a student-centric environment that bridges theoretical foundations with practical applications in machine learning, deep learning, and medical imaging.

Research Interests & Expertise

  • Deep Learning: Developing and implementing models in MATLAB, PyTorch, and Keras.
  • Machine Learning: Hands-on expertise with MATLAB and TensorFlow for predictive modeling.
  • Nature-Inspired Metaheuristic Algorithms: Designing and applying optimization algorithms for feature selection.
  • Medical Image Analysis: Specialized in diagnostic applications using AI with medical imaging data.
